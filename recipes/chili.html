Chili Recipe

When I was a fetus my family always made cheesey chili. This paragraph has literally no relation to the actual recipe but I'm writing this for SEO purposes so more people see my otherwise boring chili recipe and give me more of that sweet ad revenue as a result.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tonnes of ground beef
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 4 cans of beans
  • Handful of jalepeneo peppers
  • Handful of cayenne peppers
  • 1/2 block of cheese

STEPS

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot
  2. Dice peppers and add them to the pot
  3. Add beans and tomato sauce
  4. Stir for a few hours
  5. Top with shredded cheese (optional)
  6. Enjoy! (optional)
