Caprese Salad Recipe

This is the most delicious salad. It uses a few ingredients, but tastes incredible for a salad. Probably because it's half cheese lol.

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 tomatoes
  • 7 mozerella cheese balls
  • Hanful of basil leaves
  • Balsamic vinager
  • Truffle oil

STEPS

  1. Slice tomaoes and mozerella balls into thin slices
  2. On a large plate, lay the tomato slices out with a mozerella ball slice on each of them
  3. Add a basil leaf to each stack of slices
  4. Drizzle with balsamic vinager and truffle oil
