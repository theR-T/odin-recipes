Salmon Sushi Recipe

Holy moly!!

INGREDIENTS

  • Sushi-grade salmon
  • Rice
  • Rice vinager
  • Mirin

STEPS

  1. Cook rice
  2. Add the rice vinager and mirin. Eyeball it because you're lazy.
  3. Slice salmon into neat little rectangles
  4. Place salmon rectanges over sushi rice rectanges
  5. Sushi falls apart because you don't have the experience of a 20-years sushi chef
